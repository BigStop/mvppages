We arrive before anyone notices, and leave after everyone remembers. That is the whole trick.

Since 2018, The Copper Still has built bars inside ballrooms, gardens, lofts, and one memorable greenhouse. Staff, spirits, glassware, ice carved that morning — all of it travels with us.

Our mixologists train in the classics and stray from them deliberately. Every menu is written once, for one event, and retired the morning after.
