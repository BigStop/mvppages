01 / The House

We believe the bar is not a corner of the room. It is the room — the place every good story at your event will begin.

Since 2018, The Copper Still has served the city's quieter kind of celebration: the wedding that lingers past midnight, the dinner where the deal is closed over a second Negroni, the birthday no one quite remembers leaving.

Our mixologists train in the classics and stray from them deliberately. Everything is built to order, on site, without fuss.
