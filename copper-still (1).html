Our Story

Craft drinks,
Crafted for you

The Copper Still was born from a simple belief: that exceptional drinks and extraordinary hospitality belong together. Since 2018, we've been the city's best-kept secret for those who know that a truly great event starts with the bar.

Our mixologists have spent years perfecting the art of the cocktail — from classic recipes rooted in tradition to bold modern creations that spark conversation. We bring that expertise directly to your event, wherever it takes place.

"We don't just set up a bar. We craft an atmosphere."
