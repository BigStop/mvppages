How It Works

One night, hour by hour

Hire us once and you'll see why six hundred hosts have. Here's what an evening with The Copper Still actually looks like.

4:00 PM

We roll in

The van arrives, the copper bar goes up, shelves get dressed, ice gets stocked. You won't lift a finger — most hosts don't even notice until it's done.

6:30 PM

First pour

Welcome drinks are trayed and waiting as your guests walk in. First impressions, handled. The night officially has a heartbeat.

9:00 PM

The signature moment

Your custom cocktail — named for you, invented for tonight — makes its debut. Cue the phone cameras and the "wait, what is this?"

11:30 PM

Full swing

The bar is the dance floor's best friend. We keep pace — glass after glass, song after song — and read the room while we do it.

2:00 AM

Last call

A final toast, a quiet pack-down, and we disappear like good ghosts. All that's left is the story of the night.
